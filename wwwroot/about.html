WELCOME TO SUMMER COTTAGES

Welcome to Summer Cottages — your gateway to unforgettable travel experiences! We offer seamless cottage bookings around the world — from cozy mountain hideaways to stylish seaside villas. And for those looking to make their vacation meaningful, we provide unique holiday learning programs: language courses, creative workshops, sports camps, and more. Live. Learn. Discover. Find your perfect place to relax and be inspired today!

For over 10 years, Summer Cottages has been helping travelers create lasting memories. We specialize in working with students and families with children, offering generous discounts tailored just for them. One vacation with us is all it takes — the unforgettable experiences, comfort, and care will stay with you for a lifetime. That’s why our guests return to us again and again.

Whether you're planning a solo escape, a romantic getaway, or a fun-filled family adventure, we make it easy to turn your travel dreams into reality. With our handpicked properties, dedicated support team, and focus on comfort, culture, and connection, every detail is designed to make your stay exceptional.

Join the growing community of happy travelers who trust Summer Cottages — where every journey feels like coming home.
